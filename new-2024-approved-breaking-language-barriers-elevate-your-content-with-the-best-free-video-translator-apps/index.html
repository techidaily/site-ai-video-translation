01of 10Understand the Context

03of 10Be Mindful of Idioms

04of 10Review Regional Variations

06of 10Double-Check Tenses

07of 10Handle Gender Sensitively

08of 10Consider Sentence Structure

09of 10Proofread and Edit

Like subtitles, closed captions (CC) are words shown on the screen. They go beyond spoken words, covering all the sounds you hear, like dialogue, music, and even background noises. It makes it seem like the characters speak English right from the start, giving you a more immersive viewing experience.
