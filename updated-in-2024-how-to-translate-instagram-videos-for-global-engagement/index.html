  • Step 1. Open the Twitter app or website and find the video tweet that you want to watch with closed captions. This tweet could be in your timeline or a users profile.
  • Step 2. Tap the videos thumbnail to play the video.

  • Step 4. Look for the closed captions (CC) icon on the video player controls. It usually appears as a small icon with the letters Captions or CC inside a rectangle. Tap on this icon to turn on closed captions.
